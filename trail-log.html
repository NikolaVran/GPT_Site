Trail Log

Welcome to my trail log! Here, I'll be documenting my journey on the Appalachian Trail, sharing stories, photos, and tips along the way.

Day 1 - Start of the Journey

Today was the first day of my hike on the Appalachian Trail. I started at Amicalola Falls State Park in Georgia and hiked about 8 miles to the Springer Mountain trailhead.

It was a beautiful day and the scenery was breathtaking. The trail was well-maintained and easy to follow. I met some other hikers along the way and we ended up camping together at a nearby campsite.

Day 2 - Into the Wilderness

Today, we hiked deeper into the wilderness and the scenery became even more stunning. We passed by waterfalls, crossed streams, and saw some wildlife including a black bear in the distance.

The hike was challenging at times, but it was well worth it for the views. We camped near a lake and had a campfire under the stars.

Day 3 - Rest Day

Today, we took a rest day to recover from the previous two days of hiking. We relaxed by the lake, went fishing, and explored the area around our campsite.

It was nice to take a break and just enjoy the natural beauty of the trail. I'm looking forward to continuing the journey tomorrow!
